Banana Split Recipe

Description

Ice cream served on a banana sliced in half lengthwise and usually garnished with flavored syrups, fruits, nuts, and whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream
  • 1 scoop chocolate ice cream
  • 1 scoop strawberry ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped lightly toasted nuts
  • 3 maraschino cherries

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients

  2. Peel the banana and slice it in half lengthwise. Place the banana slices against the sides of a long, narrow, shallow dish.

  3. Place the scoops of ice cream in a row between the banana slices.

  4. Squirt the chocolate syrup on the chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry ice cream.

  5. Top everything with the whipped cream. Sprinkle it all with nuts and top each mound of ice cream with a cherry.
